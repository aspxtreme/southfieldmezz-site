Southfield Mezzanine Announces Investment in Brado

GREENWICH, Conn., November 2018 – We are pleased to announce that Southfield Mezzanine Capital has made a subordinated debt and equity investment in Brado to support two strategic acquisitions. Brado, headquartered in St. Louis, MO is an insight-driven digital marketing company focused primarily on the healthcare industry.
