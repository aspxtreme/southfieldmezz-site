Southfield Mezzanine Capital Announces Investment in The Five Star Institute

GREENWICH, Conn., May 2018 — We are pleased to announce that Southfield Mezzanine Capital led a unitranche debt facility and equity investment to support Calidant Capital’s recapitalization of The Five Star Institute (“Five Star”). Five Star, headquartered in Dallas, TX, is the largest for-profit trade association supporting the U.S. residential mortgage and real estate market with an emphasis on foreclosure and distressed servicing. Five Star produces several leading industry conferences, sponsors seven membership organizations, and publishes two trade magazines with associated online versions.
